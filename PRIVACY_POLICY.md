# Privacy Policy for Lan Lan Manager

**Effective Date:** November 19, 2025

**Lan Lan Manager** (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and manage information when you use our Discord bot.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following information when you interact with our bot:

- **User Information:** Discord username, Discord ID  
- **Server Information:** Server name, server ID, roles (if necessary for bot functionality)  
- **Messages:** Only if you use bot features that require reading messages (e.g., commands, auto responses)  

We do **not** collect sensitive personal information such as real names, emails, or payment details unless you voluntarily provide them.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used **only** for bot functionality, including:

- Providing bot commands and features  
- Improving bot performance and troubleshooting  
- Optional analytics to understand how the bot is used  

We do **not** sell or share your information with third parties.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- Information is stored securely in our database.  
- Access is limited to authorized personnel managing the bot.  
- We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
